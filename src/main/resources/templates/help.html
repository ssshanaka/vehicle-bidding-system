Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our platform

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill in your details including username, email, and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can start bidding on vehicles or listing your own.

How does the bidding process work?

Our bidding system is straightforward: 1) Browse available auctions, 2) Click on a vehicle you're interested in, 3) Review the vehicle details and current highest bid, 4) Enter your bid amount (must be higher than current bid), 5) Confirm your bid. You'll be notified if someone outbids you, and you can place a new bid if desired.

How do I list my vehicle for auction?

To list your vehicle: 1) Log in to your account, 2) Click "Sell Vehicle" in the navigation, 3) Fill out the vehicle details form with make, model, year, condition, and description, 4) Upload clear photos of your vehicle, 5) Set your starting bid and auction duration, 6) Submit for review. Our team will review and approve your listing within 24 hours.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and digital wallets. All transactions are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. Payment is required within 48 hours of winning an auction.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information. All data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent. Our platform is regularly audited for security compliance.

How long do auctions last?

Auction duration varies by listing. Most auctions run for 7-14 days, but sellers can choose shorter or longer periods. You can see the exact end time on each auction page. Bidding closes automatically at the specified time, and the highest bidder wins.

Can I cancel or modify my bid?

Bids cannot be cancelled once placed, as this ensures fair competition. However, you can place a higher bid if you want to increase your offer. We recommend carefully considering your bid amount before submitting, as all bids are final.

How do I arrange vehicle pickup or delivery?

After winning an auction, you'll receive contact information for the seller. You can arrange pickup directly with them or use our recommended transport services. We also provide guidance on vehicle inspection and transfer of ownership documentation.
